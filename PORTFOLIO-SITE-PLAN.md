## Design Aesthetic

**Style:** Modern, clean, professional but approachable

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep blue or purple (trust, professionalism)
- Secondary: Gold/yellow (energy, optimism)
- Neutral: White, light gray
- Text: Dark charcoal

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif (Inter, Poppins)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter)

**Imagery:**
- Hero: Abstract shapes or modern design illustration
- Portfolio: Actual screenshots of client sites
- About: Professional photo of Ruth
- Icons: Simple, modern (use Lucide icons)

**Vibe:** "I'm professional and accessible — not a big corporate agency, but not amateur either. You can trust me with your business."
